Psychic Game

In this game I utilized HTML, CSS, and Javascript. In this game, the computer chooses a random letter and your job is to guess it. You start each round with 9 chances to pick the correct letter. If you guess the correct letter, a score of 1 will be added to your wins and reload for the next round. If you run out of chances before guessing the letter, it will add 1 to the losses and reload for the next round
